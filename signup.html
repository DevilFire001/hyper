Terms & Conditions

1. Platform Overview

HyperClutch is a skill-based Free Fire tournament platform. Players compete in organised matches and earn cash prizes based on their in-game performance. This is NOT a gambling platform — outcomes depend entirely on player skill.

2. Eligibility

  • You must be 18 years of age or older to participate.
  • You must have a valid Free Fire account in good standing.
  • One account per player — duplicate accounts will be permanently banned.
  • Players found using hacks, cheats, or emulators will be immediately disqualified and banned.

3. Entry Fees & Payments

  • Entry fee is ₹20 per match, paid via UPI before the match starts.
  • Payment must be made to the official HyperClutch UPI ID shared by admin.
  • Entry fees are non-refundable once a match has started.
  • If a match is cancelled by HyperClutch, full refunds will be issued within 24 hours.

4. Prize Distribution

  • Prize pool is ₹800 for a 50-player match (₹20 × 50 players).
  • Prize distribution is based on final kill count and placement per match.
  • Prizes are sent to the winner's registered UPI number within 30 minutes of match end.
  • Prize claims must be raised within 2 hours of match result announcement.

5. Fair Play

  • Any form of cheating, hacking, or teaming up (outside squad matches) is strictly prohibited.
  • Match results are final as determined by in-game data provided to the admin.
  • HyperClutch reserves the right to disqualify any player suspected of unfair play.
  • Disputes must be raised via WhatsApp/email within 1 hour of the result announcement.

6. Room ID & Match Rules

  • Room ID and password are shared at 6:55 PM, 5 minutes before the 7:00 PM match.
  • Late joins after match start will not receive a refund.
  • Players must use their registered Free Fire username in the match.

7. Account & Privacy

  • Your mobile number is used only for UPI prize payouts — not shared with third parties.
  • HyperClutch may contact you via WhatsApp for match updates and announcements.
  • Your account may be suspended for violating any of these terms.

8. Liability

HyperClutch is not responsible for technical issues on the player's end (internet, device failure). We are not liable for losses caused by platform downtime beyond our control. We reserve the right to modify these terms at any time with notice via our WhatsApp group or Instagram.

9. Contact & Disputes

For any issues, contact us at itshyperclutch@gmail.com or on Instagram @_hyperclutch. We aim to resolve all disputes within 24 hours.
